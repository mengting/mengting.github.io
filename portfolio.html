ENERGY GAME 2.0

As an improvement to Energy Game 1.0 (see below), the game now works as a single screen app specifically designed for iPad with PhoneGap development tool.

ENERGY GAME 1.0

A simulation game used to help school kids learn about energy use and conservation in their homes. In the game, a dollhouse interface simulates the home environment and drives the visualizations. The dollhouse has six rooms, each of which contains household appliances that users may turn on/off/unplug to simulate their daily activities. And the visualizations reflect the energy use of each household appliance in real time.

WESTHOUSE ALIS

An integrated in-home system set of interactive control and feedback systems that supports residents in awareness of resource use and facilitates efficient control of house systems. The objective is to encourage conservation in daily activities. Currently just in demo of the UI and dashboard design...

SOLARVIS

A set of informative arts as real-time feedback for energy and power use in a solar charging station. The tool is developed in Java/Processing.
Design 1: a graphic abstraction of solar panels as calendar entries.
Design 2: a non-photorealistic rendering of an apple tree.

HOMEPAGE OF MENGTING

An experiment of personal site design with D3.js.

DRINK FOR YOUR HEALTH

A Flash based visualization of Nutrition Facts on Various Drinks. The tool is aimed at acting as a health guide for people who are interested to know how drinks and beverages differ in nutrition and affect their health.
